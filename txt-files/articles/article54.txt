Makale Baþlýðý: The Case of Immigrant Infant with Vitamin B12 Deficiency
Özet: 
Öz
B12 deficiency in children is most commonly seen as a result of nutritional intake deficiency. Immigrant children are at high risk for B12 and vitamin D deficiency due to nutritional deficiency. In this study; we aimed to draw attention to the B12 deficiency from an 8-month-old male baby of a immigrant family applied to our hospital with the complaint of diarrhea and fatigue.
