Makale Başlığı: ON TUNAY ÖZER'S POEMS A REVIEW
Özet: In this study, the poems of Tunay Özer, who has four published books, were examined in terms of structure, language and style, and mainly in terms of content. With this study, the main themes of his poems, their qualities in terms of structure, content and style, and the course of development were tried to be determined. Information about the poet's life adventure and the publishing process of his poems is given, and the relationship he has established with the tradition of Turkish poetry in terms of the language and symbols he uses is also mentioned. It has been determined that the poet, who wrote all of his poems in free verse, mostly confronts his readers with a content on "sadness, remembrance and longing".
