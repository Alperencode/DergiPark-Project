Makale Başlığı: First record of the mite family Nosybeidae (Acari, Oribatida) from Turkey: Lamellocepheus personatus
Özet: In this study, on the basis of the specimens collected from Artvin province, Turkey, Lamellocepheus personatus (Berlese, 1910) belonging to the family Nosybeidae was recorded as new for the Turkish fauna. Its morphological features were given with the scanning electron microscope images, and also distribution and ecology discussed.
